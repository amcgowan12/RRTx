Rapid Response Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 15, 2025

Rapid Response is designed to work primarily with content stored locally on the device.

Information Collection

Rapid Response does not collect, store, or sell personal information for advertising or data brokerage purposes.

The app may store limited information locally on your device, such as favorites, calculator selections, and basic in-app preferences. This information stays on your device unless you choose to share it.

Analytics and Tracking

Rapid Response does not use third-party advertising trackers. If analytics or crash reporting tools are added in a future release, this policy will be updated before those changes are introduced.

Health and Medical Information

Rapid Response is a clinical reference tool. It is not a medical record and does not replace clinical judgment.

Data Sharing

Rapid Response does not share personal data with third parties except when required by law.

Contact

For privacy questions or support requests, use the support information provided on the App Store listing or GitHub repository.
